CUTHBERT

Writing music is a challenge even for very experienced musicians. There is one side of the problem of just having to know all the harmony and counterpoint theory and there another side of using that knowledge to write something new. For an experienced musician a barrier to writing music can just be lack of inspiration (can't think of something new to try out). For someone that's relatively inexperienced but has ideas on rhythm but don't know the music theory to manifest that idea into something more. Their barrier is that much needed knowledge. CUTHBERT is a software tool we have wrote to alleviate these barriers to music writing.

CUTHBERT, Comprehensive aUTo-arrangement algoritHm for BEats nâ€™ RhyThms, is a music generator that pulls its intuition for music from datasets containing thousands of scores of varying genres. Random sampling from these datasets produces a baseline for the passage (the inspiration element), this could dictate any number of things including the rhythm, melody, or beat of the piece but can also be replaced by the user of CUTHBERT if they want a piece of music to be generated from a user provided snippet (common file type for this is a DAW file).
